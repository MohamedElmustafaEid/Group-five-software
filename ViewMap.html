Our Patients Are at the Centre of Everything We Do

HATUA is a Swahili word for action. HATUA seeks to address the global antibacterial resistance (ABR) crisis through local action, mapping the burden and drivers of disease to translate global strategy into local solutions. The HATUA team, from Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, USA and the UK has a broad range of expertise and experience, a proven track record in ABR research, and is exquisitely well-placed to address the question of the drivers of ABR in the East African Communities (EAC).
